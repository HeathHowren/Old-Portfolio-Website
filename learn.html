Learning something new isn't easy. This guide will show you where to start and what to expect when learning. Here is a video I made a while back. I recommend watching it first:


When coding, remember that the internet is your best friend (don't use google, they track everything. I reccomend DuckDuckGo). The coding language most game hackers use is C++.
This is for many reasons, but primarily due to how "low level" it is. Low level does NOT mean easy, it means that it provides very little abstraction from the computers architecture.

Note! If you do not know what "abstraction" means then DuckDuckGo it. You should do this for EVERY little thing you don't know.
